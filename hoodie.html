SLSS 2021 Graduating Hoodie
Graphic Designer | March 2020
Purpose
Our school held a competition for any student to create a design for the graduation hoodie. All submissions would then be voted on by the graduating student body to determine the most popular. I was honored that my design was selected.
The Design
I wanted to make a design that took inspiration from current trends in fashion that every graduate can feel a part of. To acheive this, I focused on three ideas:

1. Make the school identity prominant.

2. Show every course that ran during the school year.

3. Create closure with a custom diploma on the back.
