Purpose
Having recently graduated from high-school, opportunities for skill-development and new experiences have cemented those years in fond memory. What's great is that there are so many opportunities up for grabs, yet they can pass you by and you didn't even know they existed.

OppHub was created with the goal of making opportunities of all kinds discoverable to high-school students through an online database and website.
Check Us Out:
www.opp-hub.com
My Role
I was brought on as one of the founding members to help guide the brand identity of OppHub spanning their website, social media, and marketing campaigns. My goal was to help portray the classic "By Students for Students" motto by keeping our image on par with modern design language paired with practicality.
