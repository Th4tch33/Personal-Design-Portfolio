Prospective Medical Professionals

Health sciences is a study that draws the interest of many students who see a future for them in the field. As much as it is enticing, it's also foreign and overwhleming to those still deciding for a potential career. PuMP operates as a non-profit focused on bringing transparency, experience, and equitable opportunity to the field of medicine for aspiring high-school students. My job was to lead the creative team to create visuals to help PuMP achieve it's goal while looking good.
Assignment

Fulfill the role of the Co-Head of National PR by leading a team of 14 members responsible for producing branding material for social media, marketing campaigns, and online platforms.
Goal

To make every team member feel valued through creative work that challenges them to develop as a designer and produce work that is impactful for our audiences.
