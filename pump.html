Purpose
Health sciences is a study that draws the interest of many students who see a future for them in the field. As much as it is enticing, it's also foreign and overwhleming to those still deciding for a potential career.

PuMP operates as a non-profit focused on bringing transparency, experience, and equitable opportunity to the field of medicine for aspiring high-school students.
